Information we collect

What we store

  • Account information If you create an account, we store your email address, username, and any name you provide, along with your lesson and challenge progress so you can pick up where you left off.
  • Email list signups If you join the email list without creating an account, we store your email address and optional first name so we can send the lessons, challenges, and recaps you signed up for.
  • Community posts If you submit a shoutout or question to the Community page, we store the text along with your account so it can be reviewed before appearing publicly.
  • Usage analytics We log which lessons, challenges, and quizzes you view, whether your code ran successfully, and which affiliate or shop links you click. These events are tied to a session identifier and, if you are logged in, your account — not sold or shared with advertisers.
  • AI tutor interactions If you use the AI hint, quiz explanation, or tutor chat features, the code, question, or message you submit is sent to Anthropic (the maker of Claude) to generate a response. See "Third-party services" below.

Cookies

Your choices

Access, correction, deletion

You can update your account details while logged in, or unsubscribe from list emails using the link in any email we send. To request a copy of your data, a correction, or full deletion of your account and associated data, contact us using the details below.

Children's privacy

Not directed at children under 13

Code with Michael is intended for a general audience learning to code and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will remove it.
